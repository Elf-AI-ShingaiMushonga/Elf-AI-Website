Hybrid Transcription

A pure AI cannot sign a legal affidavit. Our "Hybrid" model solves this by utilizing a human-in-the-loop for verified accuracy.

  • Hardware: Plaud Note Pro for instant recording.
  • Consensus Protocol: Three separate AIs (Whisper, Kaldi) process the audio. Only disagreements trigger human review.
  • Cost Savings: Reduces transcription costs from R1.1m to ~R72k over 3 years.

ELF Law Agent

We deploy a private "Case Library" LLM. It ingests your firm's historical PDF/Word documents into a local Vector Database, allowing you to "chat" with your past cases.

  • Strict Citation: The model is forced to cite paragraph numbers from ingested documents to prevent hallucinations.
  • Dual Models: Toggles between a "Fast Model" (Llama 70B) for search and a "Smart Model" (Llama 405B) for strategy.
