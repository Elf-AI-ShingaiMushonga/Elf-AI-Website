Standard Project Process

Each engagement follows a documented lifecycle from acquisition to ongoing operations.

1. Client acquisition through referrals and network channels.
2. Client meeting with wedge pitch plus free observational session.
3. Workflow observation to identify constraints and vendor dependencies.
4. Research and design of feasible, measurable AI interventions.
5. Build architecture, validate performance, and document the pipeline.
6. Deploy with IT integration and team enablement.
7. Operate continuously with support, monitoring, and iterative improvement.

Measurable Success Metrics

  • Cost per unit of work: e.g., cost per audio hour transcribed or cost per document processed.
  • Adoption metrics: active users, usage frequency, and workflow penetration.
  • Operational continuity: uptime, latency, and support turnaround performance.

Reusable Module Standards

  • Infrastructure: shared AWS/Python/code conventions to maximize reusability.
  • Knowledge: documentation and process playbooks for each delivery pipeline.
  • Integration layer: consistent architecture for deployment and scale-out.
  • Monitoring: common observability standards for all solutions.
