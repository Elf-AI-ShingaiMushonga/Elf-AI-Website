Grand Strategy in Plain Terms

We enter with one narrow, high-value deployment. That wedge earns trust and operational access, then each custom project becomes a reusable module we can redeploy across similar clients.

Wedge First

We target one painful, repetitive process with high ROI potential and deliver an end-to-end AI implementation at an irresistible price point.

Access and Upsell

Implementation gives us visibility into workflow bottlenecks. We design additional modules from these insights and expand into adjacent problems.

Productization Flywheel

Every deployment strengthens our module library, reducing delivery cost over time, improving margins, and accelerating future rollouts across the same sector.

Execution Playbook

This sequence drives every engagement, from sector discovery to scalable deployment.

  1. 1 Choose an industry with clear pain points, repetitive workflows, and high repeatability.
  2. 2 Define one solvable, industry-wide problem and design the wedge pipeline around it.
  3. 3 Deliver fast, gain trust, and identify additional module opportunities inside the client workflow.
  4. 4 Expand with reusable modules and repeat the same architecture across similar clients.

Phase 1: Legal Industry

Target Vertical: Small to medium law firms.

Wedge Problem: Human audio transcription is costly and slow.

Wedge Solution: Adversarial check transcription workflow with human verification only when needed.

Upsell Module: Locally deployed LLM systems for sensitive legal knowledge workflows.

Current Pricing Position: Approximately 25% of traditional transcription provider costs.
